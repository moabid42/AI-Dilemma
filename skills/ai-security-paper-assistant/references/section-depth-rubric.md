# Section Depth Rubric (60-80 page thesis target)

## Minimum depth per major chapter

- Introduction: 6-8 pages
- Background Work: 8-12 pages
- Methodology: 8-10 pages
- Red Teaming: 8-12 pages
- Blue Teaming: 8-12 pages
- Final Solution/Architecture: 10-14 pages
- Contributions: 3-5 pages
- Open Research Questions: 3-5 pages
- Conclusion: 3-4 pages

## Subchapter expansion pattern

For each substantial subsection, prefer this sequence:

1. Problem statement and scope
2. Conceptual model or taxonomy
3. Technical analysis and implications
4. Example or scenario
5. Control/decision implications
6. Limitations or tradeoffs
7. Short synthesis

## Depth anti-patterns to avoid

- Definition-only paragraphs without analysis
- Long claim chains without citations
- Repeated restatement across chapters
- Broad recommendations with no operational detail
